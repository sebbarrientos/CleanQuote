About Us

Alba & Co Services delivers professional residential and end‑of‑tenancy cleaning across London. FAQs

What’s included in End of Tenancy cleaning?
Thorough clean of all rooms including kitchen appliances (inside/out), cupboards, limescale removal, and detailed finishing. First bathroom included; extra bathrooms and WCs are priced separately.

Do you bring supplies and equipment?
Yes — our teams bring everything needed. Eco options available on request.

Do you cover my area?
We cover London. Outside the A4/A2 corridor may incur a travel surcharge — contact us for a quick confirmation.

How quickly can you book me in?
We operate 8am–9pm daily. Last‑minute bookings (within 48 hours) are usually possible and incur a £50 urgency fee.

Are pets okay?
Absolutely. A £30 pet surcharge applies to allow extra time and care.

Do you guarantee your work?
Yes. If anything is missed, we’ll return to make it right.
